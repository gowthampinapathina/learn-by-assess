When students engage in peer learning, they internalize the standards expected of themselves by reviewing others against those same standards. The incentivized engagement in peer learning naturally leads to the biggest learning gains in those students most at-risk of not completing their coursework, leading to improved retention and lower cost. Team Member Evaluation

LearnByAssess' Team Member Evaluation tool is an anonymous way to evaluate team members’ contribution in any group project. Instructors, administrators, or HR professionals can use the completely customizable tool to gain quantitative and qualitative data about the dynamics of each group. Team members can comment on metrics such as Professionalism, Communication, Content Knowledge and Skills, Work Ethic, Overall Contribution, as well as custom metrics. Group members gain valuable insight that can help them improve their collaboration skills. Team Member Evaluation can be used as part of a Peerceptiv group assessment or on its own.

Collaborative Learning

LearnByAssess' research-validated framework provides a platform for truly collaborative learning. Collaboration is most successful when students with diverse perspectives push each other to improve.

With instructor-created commenting prompts, students are encouraged to provide high-quality, constructive, and specific comments to one another. Commenting on a classmate’s work, both good and bad, is a valuable learning opportunity. Students not only benefit from receiving constructive feedback, but also from giving it. Peerceptiv gives students the opportunity to act as teacher to their peers, providing a valuable opportunity for growth.

Instructor Analytics

LearnByAssess offers instructors a powerful tool to verify and improve learning outcomes through the analytics dashboard. Based on algorithms developed over a decade at the University of Pittsburgh, Peerceptiv’s data dashboard allows instructors to:

Quickly identify students in need of intervention

Differentiate instruction on future assignments

Analyze the efficacy of assignment rubrics

Track student progress through assignments.

These analytics are specifically designed to improve higher order skills, like critical thinking, writing, and argumentation.
